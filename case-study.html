The Inspiration:

The flavour ideas were inspired by Quatro's original four fruit theme. I realized I could split it up into four new modern flavours with refreshed bright colours!

As you can see above, I created realistic simulations of beverage cans including all new custom packaging with custom made graphics. I really enjoyed playing with words trying to find flavour names that roll off the tongue.

I learned a lot from this project. It was my first time putting together a brand almost completely from scratch. I really enjoyed working from a pre-established base and making something new. Quatro helped me realize my passion for brand design!

-Nikolai Meijer
